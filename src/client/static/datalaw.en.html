Data Protection

The general data protection declaration of the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich (LMU) in its current version is valid for all pages of the StruViMan project, as applicable. In addition to the regulations laid out there, the following provisions apply. In the case of any contradictory regulations, the provisions of the general data protection policy of LMU apply.

What data do we store?

Manuscripts

All manuscript-related data are stored locally in your browser. This information is retrieved using a session identifier, which is stored as a cookie.

Cookies

The cookie is stored in your browser and on our server. It contains no personal information. The cookie is used to identify your current session and does not expire.

Who do we share your personal data with?

StruViMan does not share your personal information with anyone.

How long do we store your data?

The locally stored data are retained until you delete them yourself.
